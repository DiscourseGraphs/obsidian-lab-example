

- First, find and refine a research **question** 
    - Search the literature for  **evidence** that informs the **question**, and add those to your notes
    - list **claims** from the literature that are relevant to the **question**

- ↪️ If the literature yielded an answer to your **question**: congratulations! write the answer in the Summary section. Pick a new  **question**. 

-  ↪️ If you didn't find a satisfactory answer to your  **question**: congratulations! You are likely at the edge of knowledge on this topic. 

- Formulate a **hypothesis** as a plausible answer to the **question** 

- Create a **Project** and **Experiment*

- Carry out the Experiment and collect observations as **Results** 

    - ➡️️ You are done with the experiment once you have articulated a satisfactory **conclusion** that addresses the **question**
    
- 🔁 Repeat as necessary as you develop/discover new questions
