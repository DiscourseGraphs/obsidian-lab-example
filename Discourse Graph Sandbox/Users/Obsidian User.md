## Creating a Vault

Your discourse graph can live
- alongside your existing notes as a folder in your current vault
- as its own separate vault

This is largely an organizational preference -- the discourse graph plugin can be installed alongside your current plugin lineup. This example vault ships with a selection of required and recommended plugins, including Datacore, which may duplicate the function performed by popular heavy hitters like Dataview, but no conflicts have been observed so far.
